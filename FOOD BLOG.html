Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients.

Ingredients

Base Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Toppings

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Basil
  • Olives

Recipe

Preparation

Mix flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil to form the dough. Let it rise for 1-2 hours. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the dough, spread tomato sauce, sprinkle cheese and add toppings.

Cooking

Bake the pizza for about 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

Benefits

Pizza can be a balanced meal with carbohydrates from the dough, protein and fat from the cheese, and vitamins and minerals from various toppings like vegetables.
